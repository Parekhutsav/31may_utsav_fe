Q-7 How can image repetition of the backup be controlled?

Ans:-

->We can stop repetition of the image by image-repetition property of the CSS.
->Value of the image-repetition is 'no-repeat' .
->We can also select repetition in x and y direction with 'repeat-x' and 'repeat-y'.
->Ex:-

<style>
  div
  {
   background-repeat: no-repeat;
  }
</style>
