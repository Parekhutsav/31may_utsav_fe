Q.20 What are logical and physical tags in HTML?


Ans:-
*Logical tag:- -> Logical tag is used to display text with logical style.
Ex:-
(1.) <abbr&></abbr>
(2.) <pre></pre>
*Physical tag:-
-> Physical tag is used to display some formating to the text in html.
Ex:-
(1.) <b></b>
(2.) <u></u>
(3.) <i></i>
