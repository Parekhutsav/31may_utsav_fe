Q.5 What are different types of lists in HTML? With Example.


Ans:-
-> There are three types of lists in HTML:-
1.Ordered List
2.Unordered list
3.Discription List
[1.] Ordered List:-
Ordered List is used to give order of list.
Ex:-
<ol type="1" >
<li>car</li>
<li>bike</li>
</ol>

[2.] Unordered List:- Unordered List is used to don't give any order of list.
Ex:-
<ul >
<li>car</li>
<li>bike</li>
</ul>

[3.] Discription List:- Discription List is used to give some defenition of data.
Ex:-
<dl >
<dt>HTML</dt>
<dd>HTML is a scripting language</dd>
</dl>
