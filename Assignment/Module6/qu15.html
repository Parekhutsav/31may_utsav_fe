Q-15 What are the disadvantages of using innerHTML in JavaScript?

Ans:
->When using 'innerHTML' the text is printed to the tag and old text of tag is remove and print new text.
->Some browser not fully support innerHTML.
Ex:
<div id="d1">
<p>This is some content.</p>
</div>
<script>
document.getElementById('d1').innerHTML+='<p>Appended Content</p>'
</script>
