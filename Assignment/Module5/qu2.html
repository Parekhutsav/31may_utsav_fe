Qu-2 How to embed audio and video in a webpage?

->In HTML5 we can add audio and video with <audio> and <video> tag.

[1.]Audio:-
  ->Audio tag is used to display audio in webpage.
  ->In audio tag we have such attributes like, muted,controls,loop,autoplay etc.
  ->We can add audio path in <source> tag which is into the audio tag.
  ->Like,
   <audio controls muted>
   <source src="path" type="audio/mp3">
   </audio>
  ->Ex:-


[2.]Video:-
  ->Video tag is used to display video in webpage.
  ->In video tag we have such attributes like, muted,controls,loop etc.
  ->We can also adjust video height and width.
  ->Also we can give thumbnail or poster to the video.
  ->We can add video path in <source> tag which is into the video tag.
  ->Like,
   <video controls muted>
   <source src="path" type="video/mp4">
   </video>
